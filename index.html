People can contact you if your parked vehicle is causing
any problem or is in any emergency.

FEATURES

We provide the solution for the issue regarding the parked vehicles.
You will contact the owner and inform them about the vehicle issue.

UNIQUE Features

Wrong Parking

If you are troubled by the wrong car parking, then this app will help you contact the vehicle owner.

Emergency Situation

People can help the vehicle owner if someone's vehicle is in any trouble.

Free Registration

This app is free for all users. You Just sign up and use the app anywhere in India.

OUR APP

Sign Up It's totally free

  • It is totally a free app for all users.
  • First, Please enter your phone number.
  • second, Verify your phone number.
  • Third, enter your vehicle number, choose your vehicle type and enter your name.
  • Now your sign-up is complete.

    • If parked vehicle causing any problem

  • In the first screen, Enter the vehicle number and tap Next..
  • Please choose the option of why you want to inform the vehicle owner.
  • Now click on the ping button to notify the user.
  • When the problem is resolved, tap on the done button.
  • Or if the problem is not solved, you can ping the user 5 times and each ping takes 25 seconds.

    • If there is any emergency

  • The first step is for you, please fill in the contact details of your family and friends.
  • On the first screen, Click on the SOS button.
  • Then fill in the emergency contact details and tap SAVE.
  • The second step is for emergency contact.
  • Please enter their vehicle number.
  • Then click on the upper SOS button.
  • And you can see their emergency contact details. Now you can contact them.
